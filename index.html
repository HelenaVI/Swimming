Swimming

Swimming is a sport an hobby loved by many people all around the globe. It's a sport where you build your aerobic endurance and move through the water.

Swimming Facts and Info

Swimming originated in egypt and was used for martial training back in 2500 BC. Swimming became an official olympic sport in 1896. There are 6 main strokes used in swimming, butterfly stroke, front crawl, sidestroke, backstroke, breaststroke, and freestyle.People mainly swim now to build endurance and helps helps maintain a healthy body, but also just for fun. Swimming can be a great way to relive stress and help you relax.

Famous Swimmers

Some famous swimmers are Micheal Phelps, Katie Ledecky, Mark Spitz and more.

Micheal Phelps

Micheal Phelps was born on June 30 1985 and he started swimming at the age of 87. He joined the swimming olympics in 2000 and has 23 metals in the swimming olympics.

Katie Ledecky

Katie Ledecky was born on march 17 1997 and joined the swimming olympics at only 15 years old and she has 7 gold medals in swimming.

Mark Spitz

Mark Spitz was born on february 10 1950 and joined the olymics in 1972 at the age of 18 and has 9 gold medals in swimming. There are many more talented olympic swimmers but these three are some of my favorites.
